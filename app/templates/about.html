About Us


Who are we?

Hi, we are the developers of PLACEHOLDER.
As computerscientists at the University of Antwerp, we had to make this website for our final project for the course "Programming Project Databases". In this course we have learned the practical side of using databases. The assignment was to create a webapplication for carpooling where we had to stand our from other already existing webapplications. We came up with the idea to not only select availeble options from the route but also from peoples music taste. PLACEHOLDER is the result of our work.

By Arno Deceuninck, Sam Peeters, Sien Nuyens & Tim Sanders


What is PLACEHOLDER?

Hold a place in a car, with the music you like.

This is not just another website for carpooling. It's a fast and easy way to find the ride which is perfect just for you. PLACEHOLDER doesn't only find you someone who can take you from place A to place B. No, it also finds you the person who will have a same interest in music as you. Carpooling always brings people closer together, it's a beautiful and easy was to be social with other people and with PLACEHOLDER you will already know you have a common thing: your music-style.
